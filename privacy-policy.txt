Effective Date: 13/12/2024

At FinZo Advisory, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in compliance with Malaysia’s Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA). By using our services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data as described in this policy.

We collect the following types of personal data when you engage with our services through WhatsApp, our website, or other communication channels:

Personal Identification Information: Name, age, phone number, and other identifiers.

Loan-Related Information: Loan amount, tenure, interest rates, and related data you provide during your consultation.

Chat Interactions: Messages, inquiries, and interactions with our chatbot or customer support representatives.

This information is collected to offer personalized refinancing services, generate loan calculations, and support your refinancing journey.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

We retain personal data for a period of 12 months from the date of your last interaction. After the 12-month period, we delete or anonymize your personal data unless otherwise required for compliance, auditing, or legal purposes.
